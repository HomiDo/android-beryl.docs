public class PreferGpsLocationMonitorController
extends LocationMonitorController

Configures a LocationMonitor to prefer GPS over all other methods of acquiring locations.

Features

  1. When GPS is reporting locations, all other providers are disabled to conserve battery.
  2. When GPS is not available or not reporting locations. Other providers are wakened to compensate.
